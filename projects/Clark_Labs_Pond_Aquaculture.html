Project Description:

The coastal mapping pond aquaculture project at Clark Labs in partnership with the Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation is a long-term project focusing on classifying land cover of coastal regions across the world. We utilized TerrSet and machine learning classification techniques to produce maps showing pond aquaculture, mangrove, other wetland types, and water. On this research team, I contributed to classifying scenes for the years 1999, 2014, and 2018 in China and Brazil.
