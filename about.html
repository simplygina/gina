ABOUT ME

In September 2023, I was awarded the prestigious Commonwealth Shared Scholarship to pursue an MSc in Satellite Data Science at the University of Leicester, graduating with distinction among the top 3 of my cohort.

My expertise spans remote sensing, spatial analysis, machine learning, and advanced geospatial analytics. My research focuses on mapping and monitoring environmental changes, including landfill dynamics, land cover classification, and sustainable urban planning.

Prior to my MSc, I earned a BSc in Computer Science, which provided a strong foundation in programming and data analysis. I also worked as a Technical Support Engineer, troubleshooting network issues and resolving customer requests efficiently.

I’m especially passionate about waste management, climate resilience, and sustainability. My goal is to apply geospatial intelligence to real-world environmental challenges.
